Bounce to the Summit

A vertical platformer where you bounce through elemental worlds of Water, Earth, Air, and Fire. Don’t fall, reach the portal, and master all four.

▶ Play the Game

Game Pitch

Concept: Bounce to the Summit is a vertical platformer where you control a small bouncing ball, jumping from platform to platform to reach a glowing portal at the top of each level. Each level is themed around a classical element and introduces new obstacles and mechanics.

Genre: 2D Vertical Platformer / Action-Platformer with light puzzle elements.

Gameplay: Use directional keys to move and carefully timed jumps (including a double jump) to climb. Each element changes how platforms behave such as popping bubbles, crumbling logs, moving platforms, and more.

Features: Element-based levels, double-jump system, vertical scrolling camera, retry UI and level badges, and portal transitions between levels.

Setting: A stylized elemental world divided into four realms: Water, Earth, Air, and Fire. Each one has distinct backgrounds, platforms, and mood.

Description & Instructions

Description: Climb your way up through elemental stages by bouncing from platform to platform. Reach the portal at the top to advance to the next element, and avoid falling off the bottom of the screen.

Controls:

  • ←/→ – Move left/right
  • Space – Jump
  • Space again – Double jump (slightly higher/longer)

Goal: Stay on the platforms, learn how each element behaves, and reach the final portal without falling.
